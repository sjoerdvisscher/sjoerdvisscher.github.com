Welcome weblogging friends!

Cool. Two of my best friends have entered the weblogging world.

Joeri started blogging when he went to the US. The weblog kept the people back here in the Netherlands informed about his adventures overseas. Now he has changed the language of his weblog to English: "There are some nice discussions on the Internet I like to participate in and the default of the web is in English so.. ". Joeri is a real Microsoft 'fan', as he has a lot of experience with large Microsoft software based networks that do their job very well. I hope I can point you to a lot of his down-to-earth, MS-does-work point of view arguments that often convinced me.

Marco has just installed PHP-Nuke and started building a weblog with it. (PHP-Nuke has RSS support!) It's really amazing how he has been able to create a beautiful theme for PHP-Nuke in just one day. He's one of those rare graphics designers with a true computer science background. That's a very convenient combination. Marco also created the logo for w3future.com.
