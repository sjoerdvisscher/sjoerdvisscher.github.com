UPDATED: Blackcomb Screenshots - Look REAL [Intense Beta Elite]

You can download a video of a test case UI for Microsoft .Net. It's not clear if this is going to be Blackcomb or Longhorn, but it looks really cool. Everything's moving and fading and resizing. There's a wide vertical bar at left side of the screen, much like MSN Explorer. And I like the calendar and post-it notes on the logon screen.

A quote from the video: "I signed up for $0.75 a month to be able to share calenders." Does that sound like a good price? Calculated into dutch guilders, and assuming there would be like 10 of these kind of interesting services, it sounds way too much for me.
